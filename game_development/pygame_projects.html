Games in Pygame¶


Forest fire simulation¶

Simulates forest growth, lightning strikes, and fire spreading across a 40×40 grid. Trees grow slowly, lightning can ignite a tree, and the fire spreads to neighboring trees step by step. My solution for exercise 9 from the Spring exam of 2025 Information Technology 2.

Manic Mansion¶

In Manic Mansion, the player controls a red human who must rescue white sheep while avoiding the green ghosts and yellow obstacles. Move with the arrow keys (or W, A, S, D) to collect sheep and return them safely. Ghosts move randomly, and collisions with ghosts or undelivered sheep end the game. My solution for exercise 12 from the Autumn exam of 2023 Information Technology 2.
