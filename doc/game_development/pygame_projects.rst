Manic Mansion
================================
In Manic Mansion, the player controls a **red** human who must rescue **white** sheep while avoiding the **green** ghosts and **yellow** obstacles. 
Move with the arrow keys (or W, A, S, D) to collect sheep and return them safely. Ghosts move randomly, and collisions with ghosts or undelivered sheep end the game.
My solution for exercise 12 from the `Autumn exam of 2023 Information Technology 2 <https://kandidat.udir.no/epsmateriell/eksamen?fagkode=rea3049-cs&malform=nn-no&semester=v&ar=2025&eksamensdeltype=eksamen>`_.
